Security & Encryption

Anchor Cloud employs industry-leading security practices to protect your data. The Service uses AES-256-EAX authenticated encryption, a military-grade standard recognized by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) and used by government agencies and Fortune 500 companies worldwide.

Our zero-knowledge architecture means:

  • Encryption keys are derived from your account credentials and a server-side master salt using PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256 with 200,000 iterations — they are never stored in our database
  • Encrypted file blobs are stored separately from metadata — even with full database access, file contents cannot be read
  • Each file receives a unique cryptographic nonce, ensuring that identical files produce different ciphertext
  • MAC authentication tags verify file integrity upon every download, detecting any tampering

Security Responsibility: While Anchor Cloud implements robust technical security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. You acknowledge that you use the Service at your own risk and agree to implement appropriate security measures on your end.

You agree to promptly notify Anchor Cloud of any known or suspected security vulnerabilities by emailing contact@anchorcloud.org. We have a responsible disclosure policy and will work with security researchers in good faith.
